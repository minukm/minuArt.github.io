Solar Tracking System-B.Tech Final Year Project

Main objectives of this project was...

  • To design a prototype of solar tracking system.
  • To design a project that can detect and compare the intensity of light and
  • To design a project that is able to move a stepper motor based on the intensity of light and show the voltages across two LDRs on LCD.

COMPONENTS

The major components those were used...

  • Microcontroller(ATMEGA 16)
  • Stepper motor
  • Motor Drive(L293D)
  • Voltage regulator(LM 7805)
  • Liquid crystal display
  • Sensors(Light Dependent Resistors)
